Who We Are

A Foundation Built on Compassion & Collective Action

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla. Based in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, we are a Central Government licensed non-profit committed to creating lasting change across India.

We hold 80G & 12A certifications ensuring full transparency and tax-exempt benefits for donors. We are also CSR-1 registered for corporate partnerships, NITI Aayog registered, and carry the IAF ISO 9001:2015 quality certification — a hallmark of our commitment to excellence.

Why It Matters

Every Action Creates a Ripple of Change

We believe in the power of collective action. At InAmigos Foundation, every contribution — whether time, talent, or treasure — is directed toward causes that truly matter.

  • Hunger and malnutrition relief through regular food distribution
  • Access to quality education for children in underserved areas
  • Women's economic independence through skill-building
  • Animal rescue and welfare programs in urban and rural areas
  • Greening communities with large-scale plantation drives
  • Inclusive employment through vocational skill training
